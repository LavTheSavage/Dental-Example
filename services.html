What We Offer

Our Services

From preventive care to complete smile transformations — every procedure performed with precision, artistry, and genuine care.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform Your Smile

💎

Smile Design

A completely personalised approach to crafting your ideal smile. Using digital wax-ups, 3D imaging, and mock-up trials, we design every element — tooth shape, colour, length, and alignment — before any procedure begins. You see your new smile before we start.

1–2 Sessions
🪥

Porcelain Veneers

Ultra-thin, custom-crafted porcelain shells bonded to the front surface of your teeth. Veneers correct colour, shape, chips, and minor misalignment in just two visits. Our veneers are crafted by master ceramists for a completely natural appearance.

2 Visits
✨

Teeth Whitening

Professional-grade, in-clinic whitening using advanced LED-activation technology delivers results up to 10 shades lighter in a single 90-minute session. Take-home kits are also available for gradual, comfortable whitening at your convenience.

Single Session
👑

Dental Crowns

Zirconia and all-ceramic crowns that restore severely damaged or decayed teeth to their full function and natural beauty. Same-day crowns are available using our in-clinic CAD/CAM milling technology — no temporary crowns required.

1–2 Visits
Restorative Dentistry

Rebuild with Confidence

⚙️

Dental Implants

Titanium implants that fuse with your jawbone to provide a permanent, bone-preserving replacement for missing teeth. Our surgical team uses guided implant technology for precise placement, and the final crown is indistinguishable from a natural tooth.

3–6 Months Total
🔩

Bridges & Dentures

Fixed bridges and implant-supported dentures that restore your ability to eat, speak, and smile without worry. Premium acrylic and porcelain materials ensure a lifelike, comfortable result that lasts for years.

2–3 Visits
🛡️

Root Canal Therapy

Modern root canal treatment is virtually painless. We remove infected pulp, thoroughly disinfect the canal, and seal it with a biocompatible material — saving your natural tooth and ending your pain. Most treatments complete in a single visit.

1–2 Visits
🔬

Tooth-Coloured Fillings

Composite resin fillings that match your natural tooth colour perfectly. Amalgam-free and mercury-free, these fillings bond to the tooth structure for a stronger, more aesthetic result than traditional metal fillings.

Single Visit
Orthodontics

Align to Perfection

🌟

Invisalign Clear Aligners

Nearly invisible, removable aligners that straighten your teeth without the discomfort of traditional braces. As a certified Invisalign provider, we offer the full range — Invisalign Lite, Full, and Teen — with digital progress tracking at every appointment.

6–18 Months
⚡

Fixed Braces

Traditional metal and tooth-coloured ceramic braces remain the gold standard for complex orthodontic cases. Our orthodontists are skilled in the latest self-ligating bracket systems that reduce treatment time and discomfort significantly.

12–24 Months
General Dentistry

The Foundation of Oral Health

🫧

Cleaning & Scale

Professional cleaning removes plaque and tartar that brushing alone cannot reach, preventing gum disease and tooth decay. Our hygienists use ultrasonic scalers and air-polishing for a thorough, comfortable clean every time.

60 Minutes
🔍

Comprehensive Examination

Full-mouth digital X-rays, periodontal charting, oral cancer screening, and a thorough clinical examination — all included in your new patient appointment. We create a detailed treatment plan with no surprises.

90 Minutes
😴

Sleep Dentistry

For patients with dental anxiety, we offer oral sedation and IV sedation administered by a qualified sedationist. Wake up with your treatment complete, having felt nothing — a revelation for anxious patients.

Varies
👶

Children's Dentistry

A gentle, fun introduction to dental care for children from infancy through adolescence. Our kid-friendly environment and specially trained paediatric team ensure every child leaves with a positive attitude toward dentistry.

All Ages
