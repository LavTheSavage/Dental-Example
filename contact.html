Common Questions

FAQ

Do you offer payment plans or financing? ▼
Yes — we offer 0% interest financing for up to 24 months on treatments over a certain value, and work with several dental finance providers. Ask about payment options at your consultation.
Is parking available at the clinic? ▼
Paid street parking is available nearby, and there is an NCP car park within a 3-minute walk. We're also very well served by public transport — two tube stations are within walking distance.
How do I know which treatment is right for me? ▼
That's exactly what your initial consultation is for. Our specialists will listen to your goals, examine your teeth thoroughly, and walk you through every option available to you — with honest, expert guidance and no pressure to proceed with anything.
Are your treatments painful? ▼
We take pain management extremely seriously. We use the finest local anaesthetics applied gently, and for anxious patients we offer oral sedation and full IV sedation. Most patients report feeling nothing at all during treatment.
How long does a smile makeover take? ▼
It depends on what's involved. A pure veneer case can be complete in 2–3 weeks. If orthodontics or implants are part of the plan, it may take 6–18 months. We'll give you a precise timeline at your consultation.
